## Summery of Acitvity 2.1.1-2.1.4

For the project, you will work in pairs, determine your partner, and self-enroll into your group on onQ. Decide on a team name and inventory the components in your kit, noting that not all components need to be used. Review the competition and kits with your partner, focusing on optimizing your robot(s) for a chosen strategy to maximize points rather than achieving all game objectives. Formulate the overarching design problem, identify 2–3 sub-objectives to achieve your goals, and establish 3–5 constraints based on the kit components.

## Overarching design problem

2-3 sub-objectives, and 3-5 constraints

## Brainstorm

Brainstorm ideas to meet these design challenges. For three minutes, each person individually writes
down as many ideas as possible. After three minutes, share your ideas with each other. Discuss the
pros and cons, and pick the top three ideas that your team would like to target.
